FAQs

What is BVoc?

BVoc is a normal degree that you can take after completing 10+2/12th standard. It is a 3-year (6 semesters) degree. In BVoc you will have the opportunity to work in a company while studying, this is what makes BVoc different from any other degree.

How is BVoc different from other degrees?

In BVoc it is compulsory for each student to work in any company related to his field. You will gain hands-on experience working in a company that will help you find higher-package jobs in the future.

How to choose BVoc colleges?

Ask the colleges that provide BVoc how many companies they have where they can provide internship opportunities. Ask about students' testimonials who are currently working in any internship/job.

Why does no one know about BVoc?

Because there is no awareness about it and very few colleges run BVoc courses.

Why there are very few colleges of BVoc?

BVoc is more difficult to manage compared to other courses. Because we have to deal with students and provide internships that many colleges don't accept.

What are the advantages of doing B.Voc compared to these other degrees?

After completing BVoc you will have 3 years of experience with you while other degree students will just have a degree and no experience. When you both go for an interview you will be given more preference as you have worked in that field.

Yes, you can do MBA after BVoc

Yes, it is also a government-approved bachelor's degree so you can give government exams.
